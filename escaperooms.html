DIGITAL ESCAPE ROOMS!

The front-end of a website is the part that users interact with. Everything that you see when you’re navigating around the Internet is a combo of Hypertext Markup Language (HTML), Cascading Style Sheets (CSS), and JavaScript (JS) being controlled by your computer’s browser. Using the three core languages of the web on NotePad++, the juniors and seniors developed a modern site with interactive features creating an “Escape Experience” for the user. They were divided into groups and were given the opportunity to come with a theme and individually work on their escape rooms. The escape rooms were designed to connect to one another and each time the user escaped a room, they were closer to completing the digital escape room!

By: Jimmy Martir (12), Leah Torres(12), Andre Alexander(12), and Trevor Ward(12)


You’re lost in space. You need to find the spaceship, harness the powers of the infinity stones to power up the ship, and get pass the security password to go home.
