DIGITAL ESCAPE ROOMS!

The front-end of a website is the part that users interact with. Everything that you see when you’re navigating around the Internet is a combo of Hypertext Markup Language (HTML), Cascading Style Sheets (CSS), and JavaScript (JS) being controlled by your computer’s browser. Using the three core languages of the web, you will develop a modern site with interactive features creating an “Escape Experience” for the user.
